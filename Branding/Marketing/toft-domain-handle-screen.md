# Toft — Domain & Social Handle Screen

_Prepared: 2026-05-19. Preliminary screening only via WHOIS lookups and direct platform navigation. Not legal advice. An attorney-led trademark search is still required before commercial launch._

## Verdict

**Toft passes the handle screen and partially passes the domain screen.** The bare `@toft` social handles are mostly held by dormant personal accounts (not brand competitors), but the natural app-launch handle `@toftapp` is clean across all six major platforms checked. On domains, **`toft.app` is taken per GoDaddy** (corrected 2026-05-19 — who.is's "no WHOIS data" return is unreliable for `.app` because Google Registry obscures WHOIS data even on registered domains). The SaaS-pattern fallbacks `gettoft.com`, `usetoft.com`, and `hellotoft.com` are likely available (regular `.com` WHOIS is reliable) and provide a workable URL story even without the bare `.app`.

**Recommendation (final after toft.app verification 2026-05-19):** Primary domain is **`toftapp.com`** — available, essentially free for the first year, and produces a consistent end-to-end brand identity:

- Wordmark / App Store name: **Toft**
- Primary URL: **toftapp.com**
- Social handles: **@toftapp** across X, Instagram, TikTok, Threads, Bluesky, GitHub
- Email pattern: **hello@toftapp.com** or **hi@toftapp.com**

The `app` suffix in the URL does meaningful brand work beyond just being a fallback — it disambiguates Toft-the-app from the Danish/Scandinavian surname noise, the TOFT yarn/craft brand at toftuk.com, and the small consultancy at toft.com. A user typing "toft" into Google sees mixed results; "toftapp" returns only us.

Optional secondary acquisitions to consider for redirect / squatter-prevention: `gettoft.com`, `usetoft.com`, `hellotoft.com`, `toft.co` (all "likely available" per who.is but should be verified at a registrar before purchase, especially `toft.co` since `.co` is a specialty TLD and who.is reliability is lower for non-`.com` TLDs).

Skip `toft.app` for now. Revisit only post-launch if growth justifies a $5K–$50K acquisition via the GoDaddy broker.

## What this does and doesn't tell us

**Confirms.** The domain landscape and the social-handle landscape are both compatible with launching Toft commercially. The primary `toft.app` is plausibly free, the natural `@toftapp` is free everywhere we checked, and no name-collision brand is occupying our category space.

**Does not confirm.** Domain availability via WHOIS is a strong but not perfect signal — registrars can sometimes return "no data" for short windows even on registered domains, and grace-period reservations exist. Before paying for a domain, confirm at the registrar's checkout that it's truly purchasable. Social-handle availability snapshots can change between when they're checked and when they're claimed — recommend grabbing the handles immediately upon committing.

**Still needed.**

1. **Acquire `toft.app` and `@toftapp` quickly** to lock in the screen result. Both will be cheap; both reset if a determined squatter notices them first.
2. **Attorney-led USPTO TESS trademark search** in classes 9 (downloadable software) and 42 (SaaS / hosted software). The yarn/craft brand TOFT (toftuk.com) holds the TOFT wordmark in textiles classes — coexistence is plausible because the classes are different, but an attorney needs to confirm.
3. **UKIPO and EUIPO checks** if international launch is in scope, since the most prominent TOFT brand surfaced (the UK yarn company) operates from Warwickshire.

## Caveat

Same as prior screens — public web evidence only, not legal advice. The findings are strong enough to proceed with handle/domain acquisition and to take Toft to an attorney; they are not sufficient to substitute for an attorney's clearance.
